BEE HEARD

Speak your voice

Partnered with the New Zealand Government and Parliament located at the Beehive in the Capital, Beeheard is a user friendly website which provides a platform in which you can upload videos voicing your opinions on political topics. With each video, a poll is provided, where fellow Kiwi's can decide on the extent they agree with your argument.We advise participants lease read the rules and regulations before uploading their videos. Video's are constantly reviewed by government members in order to help New Zealand become a better place and a ultimately, a better home.
